Fresno Gaming Community

I am also one of the heads of the Fresno Gaming Community. The Fresno Gaming Community originally started off being fighting game oriented, but has grown and branched out into many different games and genres. Since 2012 the Fresno Gaming Community has run training sessions, local tournaments, and major esports events such as the fighting game event Cen-Cal Standoff (CCS) which has been running since 2017 and has now achieved Capcom Pro Tour Status. The event has brought players from around the country and world to Bitwise Industries in Fresno and continues to grow each year. We look forward to more news and a post covid return in the near future! In the meantime check out our facebook group and feel free to join!
